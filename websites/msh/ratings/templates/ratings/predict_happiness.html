Predict your happiness.

Summary

Use the Somerville Happiness Survey to predict the probability that you are happy. This feature is only for Somerville residents. If enough people submit surveys to add to this site's happiness database, predictions can be made available to the general public. My program for making the model that predicts Somerville happiness is available here.

Instructions

  1. If you are not from Somerville, Massachusetts, USA, your prediction will be meaningless.
  2. If any of the survey fields do not apply to you, stop and do not click "submit".
  3. Choose a rating for each field. Except for the "happy field", all fields pertain to your opinions about your city or town.
  4. Click submit.
  5. Below the submit button, a statement about your happiness will appear.
