Submit your happiness.

Summary

Submitting happiness surveys will help us to understand happiness and make better predictions. The Somerville Happiness Survey dataset allowed me to bring happiness predictions to Somerville residents. If enough people submit surveys, we will have the data needed to understand general happiness (for people who might not be from Somerville). I will then be able to make the happiness prediction feature available to the general public. The data will be stored in a database that the public can access on the JSON API pages of this website. To discourage users from accidentally submitting multiple surveys, I added a message above the survey form that tells a user if a submission was made from their IP address.

Instructions

  1. Do not submit this survey form more than once.
  2. If any of the survey fields do not apply to you, stop and do not click "submit".
  3. Choose a rating for each field. Except for the "happy field", all fields pertain to your oppinions about your city or town.
  4. Click submit.
