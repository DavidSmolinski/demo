Learn about happiness!

On the "predict happiness" page, you can use artificial intelligence and the Somerville Happiness Survey to learn about your happiness. Click this link to see the original survey and dataset. At first, this will only be for residents of Somerville, Massachusetts, USA because the first prediction model is based on data from Somerville residents. On the "submit happiness" page, you can submit your happiness survey (even if you aren't from Somerville). If enough people do this, we will have the data to make happiness predictions avaliable to residents in other parts of the world.
