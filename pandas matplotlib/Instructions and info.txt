Pandas Matplotlib Python Project (demo for employers)
By David Smolinski

What bank.py does: 
1. It makes a dataframe (df) from bank.csv.
2. It makes the df smaller and faster by changing its data types.
3. It makes a variety of plots and saves them as png files.

Resources needed:
1. Python 3 (3.6 used)
2. Modules: pandas, matplotlib
3. files in same the folder: 
a. bank-names.txt (Moro et al., 2011) (describes the data)
b. bank.csv (Moro et al., 2011)
c. bank.py

Procedure:
1. Run bank.py to get the png files.
